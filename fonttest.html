from the CVPA Brand Guidelines

The heavy, narrow structure of Roboto Condensed Bold contrasts with the letters in the CVPA logo, yet both are clean and modern.

Roboto Condensed Bold is used for primary and secondary headers. It should always be set in Condensed Bold and all capitals with slight letterspacing/tracking. Primary headers should be put into color bars.

Roboto Regular is the primary typeface for copy text. It has a large x-height making it legible at any scale. Bold and Italic are used for emphasis. If there are multiple levels of hierarchy, other weights can be used as a sub-header typeface.
